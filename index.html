    About This Calculator

    This calculator is designed to help you estimate the radiation dose you might receive from a radioactive source. It is important to note that this is only an estimate and should not be used as a definitive measure of radiation exposure. Always consult a qualified health physicist or radiation safety officer for accurate measurements and advice.
